Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use Nudge instead of Freshdesk, Zendesk, etc?

Nudge is specifically built for small teams looking to improve their customer support by moving away from email and spreadsheets.

There's no setup or training required, so you can be up and running in minutes.

Tools like Freshdesk and Zendesk are packed with features most small teams don't need (but still pay for).

If you've got a dedicated support team and need advanced workflows, one of these other tools might be a better fit.

But if you want something simple and effective, Nudge could be exactly what you're looking for. You can try it for free, so there's nothing to lose.

What makes Nudge different than a shared inbox?

Shared inboxes can get messy, especially when multiple people are replying to customer emails.

Nudge keeps everything organised in one place, so nothing gets lost and specific requests are easy to find.

Customers can check for updates themselves from their phone or laptop, without having to chase you by phone or email.

And your team can update tickets and add comments on the go, so it works wherever you are.

What if Nudge doesn't have everything I need?

Nudge is intentionally simple, but it's evolving directly based on real feedback.

When you sign up, you'll be invited to our own Nudge board where you can suggest features, share ideas, and ask questions.

Taking this approach means that Nudge grows to meet the needs of its actual users, not just what we think you need.
